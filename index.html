Places to visit

Peak Khan Tengri

Khan Tengri is the second-highest mountain in the Tian Shan, surpassed only by Jengish Chokusu. It's 7,134m high.

Lake Song Kul

Song Kul is a high alpine lake situated at an altitude of 3016 meters. It is the second largest lake in Kyrgyzstan with an area of 270 square kilometers. It is three times bigger than the Zurich lake by surface and its maximum depth reaches just around 22 meters. Song Kul is one of the highlights of Kyrgyzstan.

Arslanbob walnut forrest

One of the biggest walnuts forrest in the world. According to legend, Alexander the Great took the walnuts from the forest of Arslanbob, and these formed the European plantations.

Osh

City as old as Rome with a history museum carved into a mountain.

Merzbacher Lake

Merzbacher Lake is one of the strangest natural phenomena in the mountains of Kyrgyzstan, and the Enilchek Glacier one of the most spectacular and rugged landscapes.
